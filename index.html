About Us

Our Chef and Staff

With over 20 years of experience Tamasan has been a Chef in some of the finest restaurants in New York City, Key West, Aspen, CO, Portland, OR and Mexico City. Tamasan is excited to present his vision of high quality prepared sushi to you and all our guests. Our caring and committed staff will work hard to ensure you have a great experience with us.

Special Events and Catering

Our restaurant is available for private events: weddings, special weekend lunches and dinners, and more. We would love to discuss how to be a part of your next event here at our place or prepare items to be served at a place of your choice.

Seasonal and Local

We refuse to compromise on quality in our restaurant. That's why we source our fish from local businesses and make our best efforts from local produce.
